Description
These are the islands from the CanvaIsland team. The islands will differ from each other in different settings and views. The first island from which everything was born, Genesis Island, makes investment sense, and all sector owners of this island will receive many benefits as the project develops depending on the level, more in our whitepaper The next islands that are expected to appear are "Lake Island", "Neon Island", "Rainbow Island" More detailed information on them will be later.

Genesis Island has all sectors available for purchase on OpenSea. The rest of the islands will have 2 types of mint:
• Random - you will get random free sectors, the number will depend on your choice (1-4)
• Specific - where you will be given a map with the ability to select sectors of interest to you.
To purchase a private island, you will need a key, which will also be an NFT. This key will open you the functionality for creating a personal island (1 key - 1 island), as well as setting up the ecosystem of the island (clouds, backgrounds, day and night, gravity, flight, landscape, royalty, building functions and other functionality). You can immediately purchase a set of square sectors, from which you can make an island of any shape. Sectors will be separate NFTs, which can be subsequently sold. In the event of a sale, all the functionality of ownership of this sector established by the owner of the island passes to the new owner. Even after the sale of all sectors, the owner of the island is considered to be the owner of the key, which later can also be transferred or sold.
